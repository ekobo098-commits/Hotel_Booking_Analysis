# 🏨 Hotel Booking Analysis | SQL + Power BI

## 📖 Overview

This project explores hotel booking data to identify customer booking patterns, cancellation behavior, hotel performance, and revenue trends. The analysis combines **PostgreSQL** for querying and data exploration with **Power BI** for interactive business intelligence reporting.

The dashboard is designed to provide management with actionable insights through KPIs, interactive filters, and visual analytics.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze hotel booking behavior.
- Evaluate booking cancellations across customer segments.
- Compare hotel performance.
- Identify high-value customer groups.
- Estimate revenue using ADR (Average Daily Rate).
- Develop an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.

# 📈 Key Insights

### Customer Behavior

- Transient customers account for the highest number of bookings.
- Repeat guests represent only a small proportion of total customers.

### Revenue

- City Hotels generate higher estimated revenue than Resort Hotels.
- Revenue is concentrated among a few customer types and countries.

### Market Segments

- Online Travel Agencies contribute the largest share of bookings.
- Cancellation rates vary significantly across market segments.

### Business Perspective

The analysis highlights opportunities to:

- Improve customer retention.
- Reduce booking cancellations.
- Optimize marketing across customer segments.
- Focus on high-performing booking channels.
